Welcome to the Department of Computer Science

The Department of Computer Science at the University of Ghana is one of the leading academic and research institutions in the field of computing in Africa. We offer undergraduate and postgraduate programs in computer science, software engineering, artificial intelligence, data science, and cybersecurity. Our faculty members are internationally recognized experts in their fields, and our students are exposed to cutting-edge technologies and methodologies. We also collaborate with industry partners and other academic institutions to solve real-world problems and advance the frontiers of knowledge.
