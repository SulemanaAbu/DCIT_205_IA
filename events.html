Events

Events of the Department

The Department of Computer Science organizes and participates in various events, such as seminars, workshops, conferences, and competitions, to enhance the academic and professional development of its students and staff, and to promote the visibility and impact of its research and innovation. Here are some of the events that the department has held or is planning to hold:

  • Department of Computer Science Online Short Courses: The department offers intensive eight-week professional certificate courses in various topics, such as web programming, information security, computer networks, and mobile application development. The courses are open to senior high school graduates, tertiary students, graduates, workers, and the general public. The courses are held online, and participants receive certificates upon completion. The next batch of courses will start from January 15, 2023.
  • Department of Computer Science Career Fair: The department hosts an annual career fair, where students and graduates can interact with potential employers and recruiters from various sectors and industries, such as technology, banking, education, health, and government. The career fair also features presentations, panel discussions, and workshops on topics such as resume writing, interview skills, and career planning. The career fair is open to all students and alumni of the department, as well as other interested individuals. The last career fair was held on October 20, 2023, at the Department of Computer Science.
  • Department of Computer Science Seminar Series: The department hosts a regular seminar series, where invited speakers from academia and industry share their research findings, insights, and experiences on various aspects of computer science and its applications. The seminar series aims to foster a culture of research excellence, collaboration, and innovation among the department's students and staff, as well as to expose them to the latest trends and developments in the field. The seminar series is open to all members of the department, as well as other interested individuals. The seminars are usually held on Wednesdays, from 2:00 pm to 3:00 pm, at the Department of Computer Science.

Events of the School

The Department of Computer Science is part of the School of Physical and Mathematical Sciences, which is one of the five schools under the College of Basic and Applied Sciences. The School of Physical and Mathematical Sciences also organizes and participates in various events, such as workshops, conferences, competitions, and exhibitions, to showcase the achievements and contributions of its students and staff, and to enhance the quality and relevance of its teaching and research. Here are some of the events that the school has held or is planning to hold:

  • School of Physical and Mathematical Sciences Career Fair: The school hosts an annual career fair, where students and graduates from the six departments of the school can meet and network with potential employers and recruiters from various sectors and industries, such as energy, mining, manufacturing, health, and education. The career fair also features presentations, panel discussions, and workshops on topics such as career opportunities, skills development, and entrepreneurship. The career fair is open to all students and alumni of the school, as well as other interested individuals. The last career fair was held on November 10, 2023, at the School of Physical and Mathematical Sciences.

  • School of Physical and Mathematical Sciences Day of Scientific Renaissance of Africa: The school celebrates the Day of Scientific Renaissance of Africa, which is an annual event that commemorates the contributions and achievements of African scientists and innovators, and promotes the development and application of science and technology in Africa. The school organizes various activities, such as seminars, exhibitions, quizzes, and competitions, to highlight the importance and relevance of science and technology in addressing the challenges and opportunities of the continent. The event is open to all members of the school, as well as other interested individuals. The last event was held on July 6, 2023, at the School of Physical and Mathematical Sciences.

  • School of Physical and Mathematical Sciences Research Week: The school hosts a biennial research week, where students and staff from the six departments of the school present and showcase their research projects, publications, and innovations, and receive feedback and recognition from their peers, mentors, and experts. The research week also features keynote speeches, plenary sessions, and poster sessions, on various themes and topics related to the fields of physical and mathematical sciences. The research week aims to foster a culture of research excellence, collaboration, and innovation among the school's students and staff, as well as to enhance the visibility and impact of the school's research. The research week is open to all members of the school, as well as other interested individuals. The next research week will be held in 2024, at the School of Physical and Mathematical Sciences.
