About Us

Welcome to the Department of Computer Science at the University of Ghana. We are a vibrant and diverse community of scholars, educators, and innovators who share a passion for computing and its applications. We are committed to excellence in teaching, research, and service to society.

Our department was established in 1974 as the first computer science department in Ghana and one of the earliest in Africa. Since then, we have grown to become a leading academic and research institution in the field of computing on the continent and beyond. We have produced many graduates who have made significant contributions to the development of the nation and the world.

Our Community

Our department is home to over 1000 students, 50 faculty members, and 20 staff members. We have a diverse and inclusive culture that values respect, collaboration, and innovation. We foster a supportive and friendly environment where everyone can thrive and grow. We also have strong ties with our alumni, industry partners, and other stakeholders who enrich our academic and social activities.

Our Organization

Our department is organized into four academic units: Computer Science, Software Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, and Data Science. Each unit offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs that are designed to meet the needs and interests of our students. We also have several research centers and laboratories that conduct cutting-edge research in various areas of computing, such as cybersecurity, machine learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and human-computer interaction.
